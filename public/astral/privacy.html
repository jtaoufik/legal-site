Privacy Policy

Effective June 6, 2026

Astral (the "App") is a PDF, JSON, image, and text utility toolkit published by Taoufik Jabbari ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the App collects, how we use it, and your choices.

Quick summary: Astral processes your files and text entirely on your device. Your PDFs, images, and text are never uploaded to us or anyone else. The App shows ads (which you can remove with a one-time purchase) and offers optional Apple/Google sign-in so a Remove Ads purchase can be restored on your other devices.

1. On-device processing

Every tool in Astral — merging or splitting PDFs, converting or editing images, formatting JSON, transforming text — runs locally on your iPhone. The contents of the files and text you process are never sent off your device, never uploaded to a server, and never seen by us.

2. Information we collect

  • Account (optional): If you choose to buy Remove Ads, you sign in with Apple or Google. We store an account identifier and your Remove Ads entitlement in Google Firestore so the purchase can be restored on your other devices. If your Apple/Google sign-in provides a name or email, it may be stored to label your account. You can use every tool without signing in.
  • Advertising (AdMob): Unless you have purchased Remove Ads, Google AdMob serves banner and interstitial ads. With your permission (the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt), AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to show more relevant ads. If you decline, you still get non-personalised ads.
  • Purchases: Remove Ads is sold through Apple's In-App Purchase. Apple handles payment; we never see your card or billing details.
  • Crash and diagnostic data: standard iOS / App Store crash reports may be shared with us by Apple, anonymized and aggregated.

3. Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not upload, read, or store the contents of your PDFs, images, or text.
  • We do not ask for your real name, phone number, contacts, calendar, microphone, or precise location.
  • Photos you pick are read only to process them on-device; results are saved only where you choose to export them.

4. Third-party services

  • Google Firebase (Firestore): stores your optional account's Remove Ads entitlement. Firebase Privacy.
  • Google AdMob: serves ads. Google Ads Privacy.
  • Apple (Sign in with Apple, In-App Purchase): Apple Privacy.

5. App Tracking Transparency

On first launch the App asks for permission to track via the IDFA, used solely to personalise ads. Choosing "Ask App Not to Track" does not limit any feature — every tool keeps working, with non-personalised ads.

6. Children

Astral is rated 4+ and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

7. Data deletion

Uninstalling the App removes all on-device data. To delete your optional account's entitlement record, sign out in Settings or email us at the address below and we will remove it.
