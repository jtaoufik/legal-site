Astral — Support

Astral is a fast, on-device toolkit for PDF, JSON, image, and text tasks. Everything runs locally on your iPhone — your files never leave your device.

Contact

Frequently asked

Are my files uploaded anywhere?

No. Every tool processes your PDFs, images, and text directly on your iPhone. Nothing is uploaded.

How do the free runs and ads work?

You get a few free, ad-free tool runs each hour. After that an ad appears between runs. The counter resets after an hour. You can remove ads entirely with a one-time Remove Ads purchase.

Why does Remove Ads ask me to sign in?

Signing in with Apple or Google links the purchase to your account so you can restore it on your other devices. Signing in is only needed to buy or restore Remove Ads — every tool works without an account.

I bought Remove Ads on another device.

Open Settings → Restore purchases, or sign in with the same Apple/Google account, and Remove Ads will be restored.

Why isn't background removal available on my iPhone?

AI background removal needs a recent iPhone to run quickly and cleanly, so it's offered only on supported devices to keep the quality high. Every other tool works on all supported iPhones.

Which iPhones are supported?

Astral runs on iPhone with iOS 17 or later.
