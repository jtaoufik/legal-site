🌱 Bloom

An addictive offline merge puzzle. Grow plants. Beat the world.

Five minutes turns into an hour

Slide tiles, fuse matching plants, and climb through 15 challenging growth stages — from a single seed to a cosmic bloom. Plays fully offline, syncs to a global leaderboard when you're online. One-tap controls, hand-drawn pixel art, gentle haptic feedback on every merge.
