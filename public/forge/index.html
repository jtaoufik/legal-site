Forge

Earn your screen time. Configure when your distracting apps are free, when they cost push-ups, and how many. The front camera counts your reps on-device — no video ever leaves your phone.

On-device push-up counting

Apple Vision body-pose framework tracks both elbow angles. UP → DOWN → UP = one rep. No video saved, no upload.

Per-day-of-week schedule

Set a different free allowance and session cap for every weekday. Strict on weekdays, generous on weekends.

Tunable cost formula

Default: 2 push-ups/min for the first 10 min, then 0.5/min after. Adjust both rates in Settings.

Glass UI

Liquid Glass on iOS 26, ultraThinMaterial elsewhere. Deep ember palette designed to feel like a kiln, not a stopwatch.
