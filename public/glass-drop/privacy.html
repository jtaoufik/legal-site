Privacy Policy

Effective May 16, 2026

Glass Drop (the "App") is a single-player stacking-block puzzle published by Taoufik Jabbari ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the App collects, how we use it, and your choices.

Quick summary: Glass Drop stores your progress on your device. It uses Google Firebase to anonymously record your scores on a global leaderboard and to gather aggregate usage analytics. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, and no tracking across other apps.

1. Information we collect

  • Game progress (on your device): your current quest level, best times, and best scores per game. Stored in iOS UserDefaults. Removed when you uninstall.
  • Leaderboard data (anonymous): Firebase Authentication generates an anonymous user ID on first launch. Together with a randomly-generated display name (e.g. "GlassCipher472"), your country code (derived from your iOS locale), and your scores, this ID is stored in Google Firestore so that the global leaderboard can rank you. The display name is auto-generated and can be edited; no real personal info is collected.
  • Aggregate usage analytics: Firebase Analytics collects standard, anonymized usage signals (screen views, session length, OS version). This data is aggregated and is not linked to you personally.
  • Crash and diagnostic data: standard iOS / App Store crash reports may be shared with us by Apple, anonymized and aggregated.

2. Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not ask for your real name, email, phone number, or photos.
  • We do not access your contacts, calendar, microphone, camera, or precise location.
  • We do not show ads — no advertising identifiers (IDFA), no App Tracking Transparency prompt.
  • We do not sell or rent any data to third parties.

3. How third parties use data

Glass Drop uses Google Firebase (Authentication + Firestore + Analytics). See Firebase's privacy notice.

4. Children's privacy

Glass Drop is rated 4+ and suitable for all audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The only data leaving the device is an anonymous Firebase ID, an auto-generated display name, a country code, and your scores. If you believe a child has provided any identifiable information, please email goloio32@gmail.com and we will delete it.

5. Data retention and deletion

On-device data is removed when you uninstall the App. Leaderboard scores are tied to an anonymous Firebase ID — to request deletion of your leaderboard entry, email goloio32@gmail.com with the display name shown in your Settings tab.

6. International transfers

Firebase is operated by Google LLC (USA). Data sent to Firebase may be stored or processed in the United States. By using Glass Drop you consent to such transfer where applicable.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy. The "Effective" date above will change when we do. Material changes will be reflected in an updated App Store release.

8. Contact

Questions or requests: goloio32@gmail.com.
