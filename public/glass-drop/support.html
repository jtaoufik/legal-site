FAQ

How is the score calculated?

Score = max(0, 100 000 − time in ms − extra steps × 200). Faster + shorter = more points. Backtracks count as extra steps.

How do I change my display name?

Open the Réglages tab → tap the Nom field → type a new name → tap the cyan check. The 🎲 dice button generates a fresh random one.

Why does the country flag default to my locale?

We use your iOS region to pre-pick a flag. You can change it anytime in Réglages → Pays.

What's the D-pad icon at the bottom right?

Tap it to show 4 directional arrows you can use to navigate the game. The grip in the centre relocates the pad. Tap the icon again to hide it.

Delete my leaderboard data?

Email thexyriox@gmail.com with the display name shown in your Réglages tab.
