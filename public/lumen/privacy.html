Privacy Policy

Effective May 16, 2026

Lumen (the "App") is a single-player light-runner game published by Taoufik Jabbari ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the App collects, how we use it, and your choices.

Quick summary: Lumen stores your progress on your device. It uses Google Firebase to anonymously record your scores on a global leaderboard and to gather aggregate usage analytics. Google AdMob serves ads. There are no in-app purchases.

1. Information we collect

  • Game progress (on your device): your current level, best times per level, and total score. Stored in iOS UserDefaults. Removed when you uninstall.
  • Leaderboard data (anonymous): Firebase Authentication generates an anonymous user ID on first launch. Together with a randomly-generated display name (e.g. "GlassComet472"), your country code (derived from your iOS locale), and your scores, this ID is stored in Google Firestore so that the global leaderboard can rank you.
  • Aggregate usage analytics: Firebase Analytics collects standard, anonymized usage signals (screen views, session length, OS version).
  • Advertising identifiers: Google AdMob may use your device's IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers) to deliver personalised ads, subject to your iOS App Tracking Transparency choice. If you decline tracking when prompted, AdMob serves only non-personalised ads.
  • Crash and diagnostic data: standard iOS / App Store crash reports may be shared with us by Apple, anonymized and aggregated.

2. Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not ask for your real name, email, phone number, or photos.
  • We do not access your contacts, calendar, microphone, camera, or precise location.
  • We do not sell or rent any data to third parties.

3. How third parties use data

  • Google Firebase (Auth + Firestore + Analytics): see Firebase's privacy notice.
  • Google AdMob: serves banner + interstitial ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers (IDFA, IP) for ad delivery, fraud prevention, and frequency capping. See AdMob data practices.

4. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On first launch, iOS will ask whether to allow Lumen to track you across other apps and websites for advertising purposes. Your choice is respected:

  • Allow: AdMob may serve personalised ads.
  • Ask App Not To Track: AdMob serves only non-personalised (contextual) ads.

Change this in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

5. Children's privacy

Lumen is rated 4+ and suitable for all audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided any identifiable information, please email thexyriox@gmail.com and we will delete it.

6. Data deletion

On-device data is removed when you uninstall the App. Leaderboard scores are tied to an anonymous Firebase ID — to request deletion, email thexyriox@gmail.com with the display name shown in your Réglages tab.

7. International transfers

Firebase + AdMob are operated by Google LLC (USA). By using Lumen you consent to data transfer there.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be reflected in an updated App Store release.

9. Contact

Questions or requests: thexyriox@gmail.com.
