FAQ

How does scoring work?

Each level gives you max(0, 100 000 − best time in ms) points. Your total score is the sum across every level you've cleared. The global leaderboard ranks by current level first, then total score.

How do I unlock more levels?

Clear the current level (cross every wall through the gap). The next one unlocks immediately. There are 60 levels in 3 difficulty tiers (Facile · Moyen · Difficile).

How do I change my display name?

Open the Réglages tab → tap the Nom field → type a new name → tap the cyan check. The 🎲 dice button generates a fresh random one.

Why does the country flag default to my locale?

We use your iOS region to pre-pick a flag. You can change it anytime in Réglages → Pays.

Delete my leaderboard data?

Email goloio32@gmail.com with the display name shown in your Réglages tab.
