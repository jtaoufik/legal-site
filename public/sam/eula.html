6. Marketplace terms

  • Role of SAM: SAM is a marketplace that connects clients and creators. Deals are contracts between the client and the creator; we provide the platform, escrow flow, and dispute resolution.
  • Platform fee: SAM charges a 5% platform fee on each deal. The fee is deducted from the deal amount before payout and is shown on the invoice.
  • Escrow: when a client accepts a deal, the deal amount is charged and held in escrow by our payment processor, Stripe. Funds are not paid to the creator until the content is approved.
  • Release rules: escrowed funds are released to the creator when the client approves the submitted content.
  • Auto-release: if the client takes no action within 3 days after the creator submits content, the escrowed funds are released to the creator automatically.
  • Disputes: either party can open a dispute before release. SAM admins review the deal history and content and decide the outcome: full release to the creator, full refund to the client, or a partial split between them. Admin decisions on escrowed funds are final within the platform.
  • Payouts: creator payouts are made through Stripe Connect. Creators must complete Stripe onboarding to receive funds.
  • Taxes: each party is responsible for its own taxes and for complying with advertising disclosure rules that apply to sponsored content.
