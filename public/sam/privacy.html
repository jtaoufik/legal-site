Privacy Policy

Effective July 1, 2026

Social Ad Market ("SAM", the "App") is an influencer marketing marketplace published by Taoufik Jabbari ("we", "us"). Brands ("clients") discover creators and pay for sponsored content through an escrow system. This policy explains what data the App collects, how we use it, and your choices.

Quick summary: SAM collects the account and marketplace data needed to run deals between clients and creators: your name, email, social handles, profile information, and deal records. Payments are processed by Stripe; we never see or store your card number. We use Firebase for sign-in and anonymized analytics. There are no ads, no advertising identifiers (IDFA), and no tracking across other apps. You can delete your account at any time inside the App.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data: your name, email address, and the sign-in method you choose (email and password, Sign in with Apple, or Google). Authentication is handled by Google Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile data: the information you add to your marketplace profile, such as your display name, bio, profile photo, social media handles, audience and category details, and pricing.
  • Deal and transaction records: offers, deal terms, deal status history, content submissions, approvals, disputes, and messages exchanged as part of a deal. We keep these records to operate the marketplace and to resolve disputes.
  • Payment and payout data: payments are processed by Stripe. Creators who receive payouts onboard to Stripe Connect, and Stripe collects the identity and banking details it needs for payouts and legal compliance. SAM stores no card numbers; we only keep references (such as Stripe transaction IDs), amounts, fees, and payment status.
  • Invoices: we generate and store invoices for completed transactions, containing the deal amount, the platform fee, and the parties' names.
  • Usage and diagnostics: Firebase Analytics records anonymized, aggregated usage data (such as which screens are opened) so we can fix bugs and improve the App. Standard iOS and App Store crash reports may be shared with us by Apple, anonymized and aggregated.

2. Information we do NOT collect

  • We never see or store your full card number, CVC, or bank credentials; those go directly to Stripe.
  • We do not access your contacts, calendar, microphone, or precise location.
  • We do not show ads, we do not use the advertising identifier (IDFA), and we do not track you across other apps or websites. The App shows no App Tracking Transparency prompt because it does not track.
  • We do not sell or rent any personal data to third parties.

3. How we use your data

  • To create and operate your account and marketplace profile.
  • To match clients with creators and to run deals: offers, escrow, content review, release of funds, invoicing, and payouts.
  • To review reports of objectionable content or abusive behavior and enforce our terms.
  • To meet legal obligations such as bookkeeping and fraud prevention.
  • To improve the App using anonymized analytics.

4. Third-party processors

SAM relies on two processors:

  • Stripe (payments and payouts): Stripe processes client payments and, through Stripe Connect, pays out creators. Stripe acts as an independent controller for the identity and banking data it collects during onboarding. See Stripe's privacy policy.
  • Google Firebase (Authentication and Analytics): Firebase Authentication manages sign-in with email, Apple, or Google. Firebase Analytics reports anonymized usage and performance data. See Firebase's privacy notice.

5. Data retention

Account and profile data are kept while your account is active. Deal records, transaction records, and invoices are kept for as long as needed to operate the marketplace and to satisfy legal retention duties (for example, tax and accounting rules), after which they are deleted or anonymized. Analytics data is retained by Firebase in aggregated form.

6. Account deletion

You can delete your account at any time inside the App: open Settings, then Delete account. This removes your profile and personal data from our systems. Records that we are legally required to keep, such as invoices for completed transactions, may be retained in a minimized form for the statutory period. You can also request deletion by email at thexyriox@gmail.com.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under the GDPR), you have the right to:

  • access a copy of the personal data we hold about you;
  • correct inaccurate data;
  • delete your data (see Account deletion above);
  • restrict or object to certain processing;
  • receive your data in a portable format;
  • lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email thexyriox@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

8. International transfers

Firebase is operated by Google LLC and Stripe by Stripe, Inc. (both USA). Data sent to those services may be stored or processed in the United States under appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.

9. Children's privacy

SAM is a business marketplace intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has created an account, email thexyriox@gmail.com and we will remove it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy. The "Effective" date above will change when we do. Material changes will be reflected in an updated App Store release.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact thexyriox@gmail.com.
