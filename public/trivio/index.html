Trivio

A trivia quiz party. Play solo, pass and play, or build your own.

Quiz night, in your pocket

Answer fast, score big, and pass the phone around the table. Play through bundled quiz packs on your own, challenge friends in local pass-and-play, build your own quizzes in the in-app editor, then publish them to a community of players and play everyone else's. Quick rounds, bright visuals, endless questions.
