Privacy Policy

Effective May 14, 2026

WakeWise (the "App") is a smart alarm app for iPhone and Apple Watch published by Taoufik Jabbari ("we", "us"). This policy explains exactly what data the App collects, what it does not, and how to reach us.

Quick summary: WakeWise runs entirely offline. Heart rate, motion, sleep stages, and your alarm history never leave your iPhone or Apple Watch. We do not have an account system. We do not run ads. We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to anyone.

1. Information stored on your device

WakeWise stores a minimum amount of data locally, in iOS / watchOS's standard secure storage:

  • Alarms you set: times, days, sound choice, smart-wake window.
  • Sleep-stage detections during your wake window: heart rate trend and motion energy, used by the algorithm to pick the optimal wake moment. Aggregated to a "wake event" record for the Stats screen.
  • Wake history: when alarms fired, how many minutes early the smart-wake triggered, the detected stage at wake. Used to power the Stats graph and export.

All of the above is stored locally on your device and is removed when you delete the App.

2. Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not request or store your name, email, phone, address, or photos.
  • We do not use accounts, sign-in, or any cloud sync.
  • We do not write any data to Apple Health.
  • We do not transmit your heart rate or sleep data to our servers (we don't operate any).
  • We do not run advertising and we do not use ad networks or trackers.
  • We do not sell or rent any data to third parties.

3. HealthKit (Apple Health) usage

If you grant permission, the Apple Watch app reads your heart rate from HealthKit during your scheduled sleep window only. This is required to detect light-sleep moments accurately. Heart-rate samples stay on your Apple Watch and iPhone; WakeWise never writes them anywhere off-device.

You can revoke this at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → WakeWise.

4. Apple's analytics (App Store)

Apple may provide us with aggregated, anonymous install and crash statistics through App Store Connect — the same diagnostics any iOS app receives. This is governed by Apple's privacy policy and is not personally identifiable. You can opt out in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements.

5. One-time purchase, no subscription

WakeWise is a one-time purchase on the App Store. There is no subscription, no monthly fee, and no upsell. We do not have access to your payment details — Apple handles the transaction.

6. Children's privacy

WakeWise is rated 4+ but is designed for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. If you believe a child has used the App and you have a privacy concern, contact us — there is nothing to delete server-side, but we will help guide you through removing local data.

7. Data export and deletion

You can export your wake history at any time as JSON or CSV from the Stats screen inside the App. Deleting the App removes all local data permanently. We hold no copies anywhere because we never receive any.

8. International users

Because WakeWise stores everything on your device and transmits nothing, there are no international data transfers to disclose.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy. The "Effective" date above will change when we do. Material changes will be reflected in an updated App Store release.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact thexyriox@gmail.com.
