Word Guess

A calm 5-letter word puzzle. Six tries to crack each word.

One word a sitting

Pick a row, type five letters, find out which ones are right. Green means the letter is in the right spot, yellow means it's in the word but somewhere else, grey means it's not there at all. Solve the word in six guesses or fewer. Quick rounds, warm visuals, anonymous global leaderboard.

Classic 5-letter rules
Every round is a fresh 5-letter word. Six guesses. Green for right letter and right spot, yellow for right letter wrong spot, grey for not in the word.
⏱️
Two minutes a round
A short, pressure-free coffee-break game. Solve and stop, or chain a few in a row when the streak is going.
🏆
Score that adds up
Each solve earns points based on how fast you cracked it and how few guesses you used. Your all-time score climbs the global ladder.
🌍
Beat the world
Anonymous global leaderboard. No sign-up. See where your total score lands against players everywhere.
