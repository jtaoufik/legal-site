Frequently asked questions

How do I play?
Type a 5-letter word, tap submit. Tiles flip to show how close you are: green for a letter in the right spot, yellow for a letter that's in the word but in the wrong spot, grey for a letter that's not in the word. You have six guesses to find the answer.
Why was my word rejected?
Word Guess only accepts entries from its English 5-letter dictionary. Names, abbreviations, plurals of 4-letter words, and very obscure spellings may not be in there. If you think a real word was missed, email us with the word.
How are points calculated?
Each solve earns points based on how few guesses it took (1500 down to 200) plus a small bonus the faster you solved. Losing a round earns zero. Your all-time total is what the global ladder ranks.
How does the leaderboard work?
Tap the trophy icon to see the top 100 players worldwide ordered by all-time total score. Your handle (e.g. "WordyLetter042") is generated randomly on first launch and stored locally. We don't ask for or store your real name or email.
What's the difference between SCORE and ALL TIME?
SCORE is the points you'd earn for the current round if you win on the row you're on. ALL TIME is the running total of every round you've won so far.
Can I remove ads?
Ads keep Word Guess free. There's no paid ad-removal option yet, but it's the most-asked-for thing on the roadmap.
The app crashed / a key isn't responding
Please email thexyriox@gmail.com with: your iOS version, iPhone/iPad model, and what you were doing right before the issue. A screenshot helps a lot.
How is my data used?
See the Privacy Policy for the full answer. Short version: progress stays on your device, an anonymous handle + score syncs to Firebase, and AdMob shows ads. We don't collect names, emails, or location.
Where can I leave a review?
If you enjoy Word Guess, an App Store review is the kindest thing you can do. It takes 30 seconds and genuinely helps the game find more players.
